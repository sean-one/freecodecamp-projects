A Tribute Page for

Quentin Tarantino

Quentin Taratino brain storming.

  • 1963 - Born on March 27, 1963 in Knoxville, Tennessee to Connie McHugh and Tony Tarantino

  • 1992 - Released Reservoir Dogs; regarded as a classic and cult hit. Called the "Greatest Independent Film of All Time" by Empire.

  • 1994 - Released Pulp Fiction; which has been named the most significant works of modern cinema.

  • 1994 - Pulp Fiction won an Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay. The film also recieved a Best Screenplay award from the Golden Globe Awards.

  • 1997 - Released Jackie Brown; an adaptation of the novel Rum Punch, that paid homage to the blaxploitation fils of the 1970s.

  • 2003 - Released the first of two highly stylized "revenge flick" films with Kill Bill Vol. I

  • 2004 - Released Kill Bill Vol. II as the second part of his 2003 film Kill Bill Vol. I

  • 2005 - Quentin Tarantino was named one of the 100 Most Influential People in the World by Time.

  • 2007 - Directed the Death Proof double feature with friend Robert Rodriguez under the collective title Grindhouse

  • 2009 - Released the long-postponed film Inglourious Basterds; a fictional alternate history during Nazi Germany's political leadership

  • 2012 - Released the critacally acclaimed Django Unchained; a Western film set in the antebellum era of the Deep South. This was also his highest grossing film to date making over $425 million at the box office.

  • 2012 - Django Unchained won an Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay. The film also recieved a Best Screenplay award from the Golden Globe Awards.

  • 2015 - Released The Hateful Eight; Tarantino's eighth film, a mystery Western. Complete with opening "overture" and an intermission in the fashion of big-budget films of the 1960s and early 1970s.

  • 2015 - Tarantino received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for his contributions to the film industry.
